About Us

Bringing more than 20 years of audio/visual expertise

3 Jay Media founder James "Jaycee" Carver began his career in audio/visual production in the early 2000s as a studio and live sound engineer for a Charlotte area band. But his passion for helping aspiring artists bring their visions to life goes back further than that.

Since those early days, Jaycee has perfected his craft through numerous personal and freelance projects. As a result of his determination to "keep working until we get it right," Jaycee has gained a solid reputation in the indie artist community and an extensive list of satisfied clients.

3 Jay Media represents the culmination of skills Jaycee has built through a lifetime of dedication to the craft that he loves.
